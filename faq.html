Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Domestic orders (US) typically arrive within 3-7 business days. International orders take 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States. International orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping.

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with tags attached. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact our support team. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Orders section.

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes.

Yes! We provide detailed size guides on every product page. Each item includes measurements to help you find the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit.

Most of our items run true to size, but we recommend checking the size guide on each product page. Some styles like our hoodies are designed with an oversized fit for a more relaxed look.

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations.

We recommend washing all items inside out in cold water and hanging to dry to preserve the quality and color of your garments. Specific care instructions are included on each item's tag.
