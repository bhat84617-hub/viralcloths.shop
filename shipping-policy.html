Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about delivery

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

We strive to deliver your orders as quickly and efficiently as possible. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping policies.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). Orders placed after 2 PM EST will be processed the next business day. During peak seasons (holidays, sales events), processing may take an additional 1-2 business days.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Times

Domestic Shipping (United States)

  • Standard (FREE over $50): 3-7 business days — $4.99 under $50
  • Express: 2-3 business days — $12.99
  • Overnight: 1 business day — $24.99

International Shipping

  • Standard (FREE over $100): 7-14 business days — $14.99 under $100
  • Express: 5-7 business days — $29.99

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package using the link provided in the email or by logging into your account. Tracking information may take 24-48 hours to update after shipment.

Shipping Carriers

We partner with major carriers including USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL for domestic and international deliveries. The carrier used depends on the shipping method selected and destination.

International Customs & Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, and fees imposed by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in the product or shipping price. Please check with your local customs office for more information.

Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is correct at the time of purchase. We are not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If you need to change your shipping address, please contact us immediately.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us within 7 days of the estimated delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and provide a replacement or refund as appropriate.
